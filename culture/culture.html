Culture & Festivals of India

Known as the Land of Kings, Rajasthan is famous for its royal heritage, colorful attire, and desert festivals.

  • Festivals: Desert Festival, Gangaur, Teej
  • Traditional Food: Dal Baati Churma, Gatte ki Sabzi

Kerala

Known as God’s Own Country, Kerala boasts lush greenery, backwaters, and rich traditions.

  • Festivals: Onam, Vishu, Thrissur Pooram
  • Traditional Food: Sadya, Appam with Stew

Punjab

The land of five rivers, Punjab is vibrant with its bhangra beats, lively fairs, and warm hospitality.

  • Festivals: Baisakhi, Lohri, Gurpurab
  • Traditional Food: Makki di Roti & Sarson da Saag, Lassi
